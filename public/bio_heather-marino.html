Heather Marino is the Sr. Administrative Manager at VasCore, the Vascular Ultrasound Core Laboratory at Massachusetts General Hospital. Heather provides managerial, logistical and administrative oversight for the growing staff, and assists with operational and procedural aspects of VasCore.

Heather joined VasCore in March of 2015. Prior to joining the VasCore team, Heather was a Clinical Research Project Manager at Massachusetts General Hospital and has worked in research at Boston Medical Center and the University of Pittsburgh. Heather earned a Master of Arts in Bioethics with a focus on surrogate decision-making from the University of Pittsburgh and a Master of Liberal Arts from Boston University, where she focused on issues in women's health.
