Kathryn Y. McCullough, MS

Administrative Director

Kathryn McCullough is the Administrative Director of the Vascular Ultrasound Core Laboratory (VasCore) where she is responsible for the operational aspects of VasCore. Katie has worked in various health care settings over the years, applying her background in operations management, performance improvement, business planning, and service excellence. Her prior roles include Administrative Director of the Center for Quality and Safety at Massachusetts General Hospital; Administrative Director of Partners Radiology with Partners HealthCare System; and Manager of Non-invasive Diagnostics & Quality Improvement in the Heart and Vascular Center at the University of Virginia Health System.

A graduate of the Dartmouth Institute for Health Policy and Clinical Practice in Hanover, NH, Katie earned her Master of Science in Evaluative Clinical Sciences with a concentration in Healthcare Quality Improvement. She also received her Bachelor of Arts from Davidson College in Davison, NC. Katie has participated in additional training in the quality, safety and performance improvement arena that includes Six Sigma Green Belt training at Motorola University in Schaumburg, IL; Quality Colloquium Patient Safety certification at Harvard University in Cambridge, MA; and Lean training through the LAI Lean Healthcare Academy at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Cambridge, MA.
