Meghna Lahiry

Administrative Coordinator

Meghna joined Vascore in October 2015. She graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a Bachelors of Science in Education and is currently pursuing a Masters of Healthcare Administration from Suffolk University. Prior to working at Vascore, she worked as an Administrative Assistant at Newton Wellesley Hospital’s Rehabilitation Services Center in 2011 and joined Brigham and Women’s Fish Center for Women’s Health in 2014. Meghna is the Administrative Coordinator at Vascore and manages front office coordination at both Vascore and VTEC. She supports the Administrative Manager in daily tasks related to clinical trials within Vascore and VTEC’s event management and planning. Meghna is an avid music enthusiast and she sings with the Metropolitan Chorale of Brookline.
