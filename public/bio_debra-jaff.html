Debra A. Jaff

Business Manager

Debra A. Jaff is the Administrative Director of VasCore, the Vascular Ultrasound Core Laboratory. Debra honed her management and operational skills, managing a small law firm in New York City. In 1987, Debra became Director of Marketing for a large multi-city law firm. Subsequently, Debra assumed responsibility for the organizational, legal and financial aspects of VasCore. Customer satisfaction has always been Debra’s focus, and with the increasing international involvement of VasCore, Debra continues to develop processes to insure excellence in infrastructure so that the technical expertise of VasCore remains unparalleled.
