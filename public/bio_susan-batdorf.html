Susan M. Batdorf

Quality Auditor

As our Quality Auditor, Susan ensures VasCore delivers the highest level of accuracy by regularly auditing our internal systems, data and final reports for clinical studies. With 18 years’ experience as a Quality Auditor, Susan has a vast knowledge of regulatory guidelines everywhere from North America to Eastern Europe to Asia. She holds a Bachelors of Science from the University of Illinois and a Masters of Science in Health Education from George Williams College. Before her career in quality auditing, she taught biology and has used this training in education to help develop a Regulatory Oversight and Compliance class at Northwestern University. Susan is also involved in many professional organizations, including the Society of Clinical Research Associates (SOCRA) and the Society of Quality Assurance.
